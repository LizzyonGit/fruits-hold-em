How to play

Fruits hold 'em is a fruit machine to test your luck. Get 3 similar fruits in the middle row, and you win credit. See how far you can get!

Start

You start with 10 credit. Click GO to start spinning. Each game consists of 3 spins, and costs 5 credit. If you get 3 similar fruits in the middle row, you getb 10 credit. After 3 spins, a new game starts. When you have no more credit for a new game, the play restarts.

Hold

Each column has a HOLD button under it. Click the HOLD button to save the fruit and prevent spinning of the held column. You can hold up to 2 columns each spin.

Restart

If you feel you're not doing to well, click RESTART to try a new game.
